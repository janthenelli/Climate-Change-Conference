About CCA Conference

The beautiful city of Portland, Oregon will be the host city for Climate Change Awareness Conference!

Explore the future of climate change related policy with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover how you can do your part to keep our big green planet green!

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international climatology community and share insight and ideas with some of the world's top experts, scientists, and environmentalists? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of climate change.

Climate Change Conference is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

Reducing Carbon Footprint: A shift towards renewable energy Protected Forests and Wildlands: Protecting our Earth's lungs Cleaning Our Oceans: And keeping them clean Polar Ice Loss: Preparing for the worst How It Affects Weather: Stronger, more dangerous storms? Paris Climate Agreement: Coming together as one

Speakers

Speaker 1

Speaker 1 is a climatologist and professor who is passionate about renewable energy and educating people on its many benefits.

Speaker 2

Speaker 2 is a forester and volunteers at national parks across the United States to give educational tours and help keep our parks clean.

Speaker 3

Speaker 3 is an oceanographer currently working on solutions to help protect and save the Great Barrier Reef. She also supports many organizations and groups dedicated to keeping our oceans clean.

Speaker 4

Speaker 4 is a meteorologist who has dedicated much of her career working with scientists on ways to preserve habitats affected by polar ice melting.

Speaker 5

Speaker 5 is also a meteorologist who has spent the past decade studying weather phenomena patterns and how climate change, rising sea levels, and rising temperatures have impacted them.

Speaker 6

All of his professional life, Speaker 6 has worked with the United Nations, G7, and various other world organizations trying to bring awareness and policy change to the global scene in order to combat climate change.
